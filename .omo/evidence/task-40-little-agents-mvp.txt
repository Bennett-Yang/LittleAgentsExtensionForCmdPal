- Runtime settings are passed from `LittleAgentsExtensionCommandsProvider` to `AgentsListPage`, then to `AgentRunCommand`, then to `ChatRunPage`.

- Valid values in the inclusive 0.0 to 2.0 range are passed to chat requests.
- System prefix text is read from settings and prepended by the existing `ChatRunPage.BuildRequest()` runtime-settings path.
